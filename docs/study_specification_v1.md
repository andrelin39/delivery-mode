# Study specification v1

## Study Design

Retrospective cohort study

## Setting

Changhua Christian Hospital  
Tertiary referral medical center  
Taiwan

## Study Context

Strict COVID-19 admission management period

## Study Period

2021-08-17 to 2022-12-31

## Final Analytic Cohort

N = 1364

## Primary Exposure

Labor admission pathway

- Symptomatic admission
- Planned induction admission

## Primary Outcome

Cesarean delivery

## Secondary Outcomes

- Operative delivery
- Mode of birth
- Birth weight
- Apgar scores
- Low Apgar indicators
- Labor duration

## Covariates

- Age
- BMI
- Gestational age
- Birth weight
- Gravidity
- Parity

## Sensitivity Analysis

Exclude records with `FLAG_LABOR_ONSET_REQUIRES_REVIEW == 1`.

## Freeze Status

This specification is frozen as v1 for the Midwifery manuscript work package. Any future change should be documented as an explicit protocol or manuscript-specification revision.
