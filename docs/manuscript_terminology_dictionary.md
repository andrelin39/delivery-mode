# Manuscript terminology dictionary

## Required Manuscript Terms

Use the following terms consistently in the Midwifery manuscript:

- Labor admission pathway
- Symptomatic admission
- Planned induction admission
- Cesarean delivery
- Operative delivery

## Terms To Avoid

Do not use the following terms in the main manuscript text:

- spontaneous labor
- non-spontaneous labor
- labor onset pathway

Exception: these terms may be used in the Methods section only when explaining PGADM source coding, derivation logic, or the relationship between source variables and manuscript-facing terminology.

## Mapping

| Manuscript term | Analysis coding context |
| --- | --- |
| Symptomatic admission | PGADM-derived admission with labor signs |
| Planned induction admission | PGADM-derived admission without labor signs and managed as induction admission |
| Cesarean delivery | Cesarean section outcome |
| Operative delivery | Cesarean delivery or vacuum extraction delivery |
